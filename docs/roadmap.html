How to Use This in Customer Conversations

💡 ICM Positioning

ICM has no confirmed date — do not quote one. The safe framing: "ICM is on the roadmap. Once T&Q is live and your quota data is structured correctly, you'll be first in line when it ships." This keeps the conversation open without making a commitment you can't keep.

💡 T&Q V3 — May 2026

May 2026 is the current target for T&Q V3. This is the most specific date on the roadmap — but it's still a target. Confirm with the Apps team before using it in any customer proposal or SOW. V3 is a breaking change — factor in migration effort for any customer currently on V2.

📝 Roadmap Policy

Anaplan's roadmap is directional. Feature timing can shift based on engineering priorities, customer feedback, and platform dependencies. Never use roadmap items to close a deal. Use them to frame the long-term vision and keep the customer engaged post-go-live.
