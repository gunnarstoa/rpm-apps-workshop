Territory & Quota (T&Q) Application Lab Guide

T&Q Module T&Q V3 Workshop

About This Workshop

The RPM Apps Technical Enablement Workshop is a full-day, hands-on session designed for Anaplan delivery practitioners — Solutions Consultants, Solution Architects, and Technical Consultants — who will be implementing or supporting the Territory & Quota (T&Q) application for customers.

This workshop covers the complete T&Q implementation lifecycle: from initial discovery and the Anaplan Way methodology, through the configurator tool, post-generation steps, data integration, the spoke app user experience, and common extensions. By the end of the T&Q Module, participants will have hands-on experience with the configurator and a clear picture of what it takes to deliver a successful T&Q implementation.

The T&Q application is the cornerstone of the Anaplan RPM (Revenue Performance Management) Apps suite and is consistently the highest-demand, highest-value application in the portfolio. A strong T&Q skillset is foundational to the broader SPM practice.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this workshop, participants will be able to:

  1. Understand the SPM landscape — articulate the five SPM pillars, explain how T&Q fits into the broader Revenue Performance Management picture, and identify the strongest landing scenarios for T&Q.
  2. Navigate the configurator — walk through all 14 configurator pages, make informed decisions on each setting, and understand which choices are difficult to reverse post-generation.
  3. Understand and execute post-generation steps — apply the post-gen checklist, identify and resolve known issues, and assess model readiness for customer handoff.
  4. Walk a customer through the spoke app — guide a customer through all major sections of the T&Q spoke application, explaining each workflow and data view in business terms.
  5. Identify and scope common extensions — recognize when base app functionality meets a requirement vs. when a net-new extension is needed, and provide T-shirt size estimates for the most common extension types.

Prerequisites

To get the most out of this workshop, participants should have:

  • Basic Anaplan platform knowledge — familiarity with modules, lists, dashboards, and model building concepts. This is not an Anaplan 101 session.
  • Completed Anaplan Level 1 certification (or equivalent platform experience)
  • Access to an Anaplan workspace — you will need a workspace with at least one model to run the configurator during the hands-on lab. Confirm with your manager ahead of time.
  • Reviewed the T&Q solution brief — available on the internal enablement portal
  • Downloaded the lab dataset files — see the Configurator Exercise section for the CloudWorks data files. Have these ready before the afternoon lab session.
⚠ Important

Do not run the configurator app generation against a customer workspace during this workshop. The exercise uses a dedicated training workspace. Generating a T&Q app creates ~8,000 objects and cannot be easily undone.
